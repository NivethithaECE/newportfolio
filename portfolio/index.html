Projects

HERBSPOT

Our project, Herbspot, aims to revolutionize the herb-buying experience by connecting buyers directly with sellers in the field,eliminating intermediaries. Through our app, users can easily access information about the availability, location, and pricing of various herbs. This enables buyers to conveniently search for herbs near their location and place orders based on their specific requirements. By facilitating direct and fresh transactions between buyers and sellers,Herbspot not only reduces the involvement of intermediaries but also lowers prices, providing a more affordable and efficient herb-buying experience

SECE college Bus ticket booking system

The SECE College Bus Ticket Booking System is a web application designed exclusively for hostellers. It streamlines the process of booking bus tickets for hostellers who choose college buses when traveling home. By utilizing our website, hostellers can conveniently book their bus tickets in advance, eliminating last-minute rushes and the risk of not getting a seat.
